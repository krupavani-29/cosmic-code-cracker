About

Welcome to the Cosmic Code Cracker, an exciting riddle-solving competition organized by ACM VVIT! Test your wit and logic by solving 50 cosmic-themed riddles. Each riddle comes with a hint to guide you, but only the sharpest minds will crack them all!

Rules

- Each player can participate only once using their registered roll number.
- You must solve 8 riddles to complete the game.
- Enter the answer to the current riddle to unlock the next one.
- You have 20 minutes from login to complete the game.
- No external help or tools are allowed.

Scoring

- You start with 100 points per riddle.
- For each second you take to solve a riddle, 1 point is deducted (minimum 10 points per riddle).
- Your total score is the sum of points from all 50 riddles.
- The faster you solve, the higher your score!

Prizes

- 1st Place: Participation certificate and winning certificate + Price money
- 2nd Place: Participation certificate and winning certificate
- 3rd Place: Participation certificate and winning certificate
- Top 10 players will receive a special ACM VVIT certificate!
